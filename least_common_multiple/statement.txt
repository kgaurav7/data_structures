Problem Introduction The least common multiple of two positive integers a and b is the least positive integer m that is divisible by both a and b.
 
Problem Description
Task: Given two integers a and b, find their least common multiple.
Input Format: The two integers a and b are given in the same line separated by space.
Constraints: 1 = a, b = 2 · 109 .
Output Format. Output the least common multiple of a and b.
